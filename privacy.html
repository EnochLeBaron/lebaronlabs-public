Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Information We Collect

LeBaronLabs apps may collect account information needed to operate the service, such as name, email address, profile information, tasks, projects, shared project membership, messages, uploaded files, and user preferences.

Calendar Feed Data

If you connect an iCal calendar feed in LeBaronLabs Task Manager, the app reads that feed to display calendar events in the Calendar tab and calendar boxes.

We may store calendar sync settings, feed URLs, and synced event data so the app can refresh and display your events. We do not create, edit, or delete events in external calendars, and we do not sell calendar feed data.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide app features, authenticate users, sync and display task and calendar data, support collaboration, maintain security, and improve reliability.

Sharing

Project and message data may be shared with users you add to shared projects or conversations. Calendar feed data connected through your account is shown only to you in Task Manager by default.

Retention and Removal

Data is retained while your account or workspace uses the app. You can remove calendar feed connections in Task Manager or contact support to request removal of stored calendar sync data.
