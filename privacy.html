Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Information We Collect

LeBaronLabs apps may collect account information needed to operate the service, such as name, email address, profile information, tasks, projects, shared project membership, messages, uploaded files, and user preferences.

Google Calendar Data

If you connect Google Calendar in LeBaronLabs Task Manager, the app requests read-only access to display your calendar events in the Calendar tab and calendar boxes.

We may store calendar sync settings, OAuth tokens, and synced event data so the app can refresh and display your events. We do not create, edit, or delete Google Calendar events, and we do not sell Google Calendar data.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide app features, authenticate users, sync and display task and calendar data, support collaboration, maintain security, and improve reliability.

Sharing

Project and message data may be shared with users you add to shared projects or conversations. Google Calendar data connected through your account is shown only to you in Task Manager by default.

Retention and Removal

Data is retained while your account or workspace uses the app. You can revoke Google Calendar access from your Google Account permissions page or contact support to request removal of stored Google Calendar sync data.
